history

Seshadripuram College in Malleswaram, Bangalore, was established in 1973.
It is part of the larger Seshadripuram Educational Trust, which was founded in 1930 by two women, Anandamma and Seethamma. The trust runs numerous educational institutions, including the flagship Seshadripuram College.

courses

UG programs include B.Com, BBA, BCA, and B.Sc, while PG programs include M.Com, MBA, and MCA

campus
